#concept 

A [[PagerDuty]] internal [[carreer/development/programming/message/Event|Event]] that capture at least one [[PagerDuty Alert|Alert]]. It indicates [[carreer/development/programming/Service|Service]] disruption and prompts [[PagerDuty Notification|notification]] based on [[PagerDuty Urgency|Urgency]] and [[PagerDuty Priority]]
