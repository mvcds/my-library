The amount of work a [[system]] can process per unit of [[time]] — for example, requests per second, transactions per second, or bytes per second.

It is one of the main [[Performance]] [[Metric|metrics]] for [[Computer|computers]], [[Network|networks]], and [[Distributed System|distributed systems]], and is often discussed alongside [[Latency]] as a complementary measure.

---

In a team/process context, see [[carreer/lean/agile/Throughput|Throughput]] as a [[Lean]] indicator of work items completed.
