#concept in [[2 - Introduction]]

The disposition of winners who believe their [[Success]] is entirely their own doing. Once that belief sets in, [[gratitude]] and [[humility]] become impossible: as they do not even acknowledge the [[Invisible Support Network]].

The consequence is [[Civic Sensibility|civic]]: those who owe nothing to anyone feel no pull toward the [[Common Good]], and lose the capacity to see fellow citizens, especially the [[Success|losers]] of the same [[system]], as anything other than people who simply didn't [[Meritocracy|measure up]].
