#concept in [[2 - Introduction]]

Under [[Meritocracy]], any shortfall[^1] is read as the person's own fault. [[Success|Failure]] stops being an [[maths/probability-theory/Event|Event]] and becomes a verdict.

This is a contributor to the [[Meritocratic Anxiety]]: the [[cost]] of falling short is not just the [[maths/probability-theory/Event|Outcome]] but the [[Moral]] reading of oneself as the kind of person who deserved to fall short.

[^1]: A rejection, a lost job, a stalled career, etc
