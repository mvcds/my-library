#concept in [[2 - Introduction]]

The capacity to see oneself as bound to fellow citizens: to feel that the [[Common Good]] has a real claim on one's life. It is what makes [[Solidarity]] more than a slogan: a quiet sense that other people's fates and one's own are not separate ledgers.

In a [[Meritocracy]], this sensibility is what the [[Hubris of Meritocratic Success]] hollows out. When winners owe nothing and losers deserve their lot, the felt bond between citizens thins until there is nothing left to appeal to.
