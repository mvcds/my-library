In the USA, using [[money]] to be admitted into a [[Common Good|public, elite university]] is not necessarily [[legal|illegal]]. Donations, family history, favors, and influence can be used to make that happen.

If a crime, such as bribing[^1], is made for that admission, then it is a problem.
