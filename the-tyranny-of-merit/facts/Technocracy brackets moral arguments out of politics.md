[[Technocracy]] is used as a way to organize the [[Common Good|Public Good]], and by treating public questions as technical [[Optimization|optimizations]], it brackets [[Moral]] arguments out of [[Politics]], as personal [[Value|value]] get reframed as [[Ideology|ideologically]] suspect. Giving only [[Credential|credentialed]] experts the capacity to opine.

[[Globalization]] both relies on technocracy and reinforces it, producing cosmopolitan [[environment|environments]] that erode the standing of local [[culture|customs]] and weaken belonging to a [[Tribe|group]].
