#fact in [[3 - Winners and Losers]]

Americans have long tolerated a wide [[Equality|inequality]] of [[Outcome|outcomes]] on the premise of the [[American Dream]] — that working hard and playing by the rules buys upward [[Social Mobility|mobility]]. That bargain no longer pays out: moving up is harder than ever, and the [[The richest 1% get more money than the bottom half combined|stark gap between top and bottom]] now reads not as the [[Cost of Opportunity|cost of opportunity]] but as proof the contest is rigged.
