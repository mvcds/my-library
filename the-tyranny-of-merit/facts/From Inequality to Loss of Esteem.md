#fact in [[3 - Winners and Losers]]

[[Protest|Protests]] often begin as complaints about material [[Equality|inequality]], but they don't stay there. They migrate toward grievances about [[Social Esteem]] and standing — the sense of having been written off by the [[Winner|winners]]. The [[economics|economic]] wound is real, but the [[Moral|moral]] wound — being deemed less worthy — is what fuels the durable anger.
