Parents, driven by [[Meritocratic Anxiety]] adapt to their [[Environment]] by managing the child's studying up close. Not due to devotion, but due to making sure their kids are [[3 - Winners and Losers|Winners]].

[[Money]] gives families more [[Asset|Resources]] to create the appearance of earned [[Success]]. What looks like investment in [[Knowledge|education]] is a defensive crouch against the [[Individualization of Failure|Individualized Failure]].
