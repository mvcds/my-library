#fact in [[1 - Prologue]]

[[Globalization]] allowed us to not dependent on local [[economics|economies]] as we once were. Those positioned to benefit from [[Global Market|Global Markets]] have distanced themselves from those who can't which eroded the everyday mutual reliance that used to bind neighbors across class lines.

The author does not present this neutral economic shift but as a precondition for the collapse of [[Solidarity]]: once the [[Winner|well-off]] no longer need the local butcher, school, or factory, they have no material stake in the welfare of those who do.
