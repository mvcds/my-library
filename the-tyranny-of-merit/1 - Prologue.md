During the [[covid]] [[pandemic]], caring for others meant quarantining yourself, a [[Solidarity]] out of [[fear]] rather than a common cause. The slogan "we're all in this together" didn't land the same for everyone: the experience of the shared [[crisis]] was already stratified.

People who can benefit from [[Global Market|Global Markets]] have distanced themselves on those who can't, weakening our dependence on local [[Economics|economies]], this has shifted the [[Politics|political]] divide from "left vs right" to "[[Open vs Closed]]".

And tho the "open" side wants everyone to get the same [[Probability|chances]] so [[Meritocracy]] would be able to do its magic, being on the "winner" side of anything makes someone believe they've deserved it, regardless of the [[Invisible Support Network]]. That blindness is the entry point for [[The tyranny of merit]].
